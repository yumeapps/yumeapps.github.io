Danmaku Implosion

Fight through 6 bosses (and counting) in three different difficulties. Full levels, intricate scoring system, secrets, powerups, slow-mode, and bombs. Danmaku games at their finest.

Swim Calculator

Create, email, and share swim workouts. View preloaded workouts or look at and share community workouts online. Other features include tracking time on events, calculating weekly, monthly, and yearly distances, and being able to quickly editing and save workouts.

Learn English Now

Educational app for young children or ESL students. Hundreds of vocabulary words in six categories. View charts to see your child's progress.

Sector 11

Battle as Aya in this top-down danmaku through 100 mini levels. Why is there an influx of invaders arriving in Sector 11? What's going on in the other sectors?

Danmaku Initiate

Back to the basics. The first Danmaku game released by Yume Apps. It's not as good as the newer Danmaku games, but battle through 5 stages and 15 boss fights in four difficulties. Boss rush mode and solo boss fights!
